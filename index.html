My Cat

Several years ago my mom and I met a kitten at the edge of the woods who came running when we asked if she was hungry. She rode home in my lap and Miel has lived happily with us ever since. She loves to play, scratch, and sleep all around the house. Sometimes it seems like she can understand English…
